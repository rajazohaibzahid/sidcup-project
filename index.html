WELCOME TO SIDCUP FAMILY GOLF!

Sidcup Family Golf is a Toptracer driving range and crazy golf venue in Sidcup, South East London. Passionate about technology, player development and making golf fun and accessible to everyone.

ABOUT US

Home to a 46-bay, multi-tier, floodlit driving range, powered by Toptracer Range technology.
Complimented by a practice green and bunker, coffee shop and American Golf Store.
There truly is something for everyone as we also boast two outdoor 18-hole dinosaur themed crazy golf courses.
Please note: we are a cashless venue.
The range closes at 10pm with last balls at 9pm.
